OURRESEARCH AREAS

Moleular, Cell and Development Biology
Research groups in molecular, cellular, and developmental biology investigate the mechanisms of biological processes across a range of organisms, from viruses and microbes to plants, worms, fish and mammals.
ENVIRONMENT AND HEALTH
The environmental health research group focuses on health and disease mechanism and its relation with the environment.
BIORESOURCE AND AGRICULTURE
The main research areas are bioresource management and plant or crop responses to adverse environments for enhancing crop production.
